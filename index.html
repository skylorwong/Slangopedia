Motivation

In general, studying language is important because it serves as the foundation of human communication and connection, allowing people to express their thoughts, feelings, and ideas. By studying language, we can gain important insights into how people connect and engage with the world around them.

In particular, studying slang can be insightful because it provides a window into social and cultural trends, and also reflects how people interact on a casual or more informal level.

Goal

The goal of Slangopedia is to analyze Urban Dictionary slang through interactive visualizations that allow people to explore its dynamic, linguistic, and social dimensions.

Some questions that Slangopedia aims to address:

1. What are the characteristics of Urban Dictionary slang and what does that reveal about how people communicate with each other?
2. Is Urban Dictionary slang connected to positive or negative contexts? What specific emotions are evoked?
3. How has Urban Dictionary slang changed over time?

Method

Slangopedia combines traditional data analysis methods with machine learning NLP techniques.
